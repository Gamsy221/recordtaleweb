There’s something heartbreaking about realizing the stories are gone.

The way your dad used to tell that story about his first job.
The way your mom laughed when she talked about how she met your father.
The tiny details that never make it into photo albums.

Too often, we try to preserve stories when it’s already too late - when memories blur and details slip away.

Recordtale was built around one simple belief:

Wouldn’t you rather hear someone’s legacy while they’re still living it?
